Overview & Challenge

Transforming complex NASA Earth data into actionable climate insights for communities worldwide

The Problem

Generic weather apps fail to provide the contextual, actionable climate insights needed for local adaptation, leaving communities vulnerable to heat stress, poor air quality, and extreme weather events.

The Solution

TerraPulse is a mobile app that synthesizes NASA APIs (POWER, GPM, MODIS) and real-time feeds to provide personalized, localized risk assessments, calculating how the environment feels and its risk to your specific activity.

Our Goal

To empower every individual with data-driven environmental awareness. Our goal is global‚Äîto scale TerraPulse beyond South Asia to every community facing climate change, creating a healthier, more adaptable population worldwide.

Data & Impact

NASA Data Integration

NASA POWER API

Used for solar radiation, temperature, and wind data, vital for calculating Heat Stress Index and farming suitability.

GPM API

Analyzes rainfall intensity for localized "very wet" condition alerts.

MODIS Air Quality & Aerosol Data

Provides real-time pollution levels for Health Safety Advisories.

Earth Observatory Datasets

Visualizes long-term trends for climate education and long-term planning.
